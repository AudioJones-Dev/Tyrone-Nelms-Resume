## Voice & Tone

- **Plain over impressive.** Say "fix the follow-up process," not "optimize the revenue lifecycle."
- **Operator's voice.** First-hand, concrete, calm. Every abstract claim gets a concrete example within one sentence.
- **Evidence-humble.** Comfortable saying "that metric isn't verified yet." It builds more trust than the metric would.
- **No hype vocabulary.** Avoid: revolutionary, cutting-edge, 10x, unlock, supercharge.
