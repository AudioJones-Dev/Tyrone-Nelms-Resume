# Target Roles

This file defines target roles and the resume strategy for each audience.

## Tier 1 — Highest Alignment

These roles best match the current professional direction.

### AI Operations Consultant

Emphasize:

- AI workflow design
- Business process automation
- Operations consulting
- CRM and customer experience systems
- Founder-led service business advisory
- Digital transformation
- Knowledge management

### Operations Consultant

Emphasize:

- Process improvement
- SOP development
- Workflow design
- Client operations
- Service delivery
- Cross-functional execution

### Digital Transformation Consultant

Emphasize:

- Technology adoption
- CRM and automation systems
- AI-assisted workflows
- Project management
- Customer experience modernization

### Business Systems Consultant

Emphasize:

- Workflow mapping
- Tool implementation
- CRM systems
- Documentation
- Process architecture
- Automation strategy

## Tier 2 — Strong Fit

### Operations Manager

Emphasize:

- Team leadership
- Daily operations
- Productivity improvements
- Customer service operations
- Field and administrative coordination

### Project Manager

Emphasize:

- Planning and execution
- Stakeholder communication
- ClickUp, Notion, Asana
- Cross-functional delivery
- Documentation and reporting

### Customer Success Manager

Emphasize:

- Enterprise customer operations
- Escalation handling
- CRM systems
- Account support
- Customer satisfaction
- Provider/customer communication

### Solutions Consultant

Emphasize:

- Discovery
- Business problem diagnosis
- Technical translation
- Customer workflows
- Implementation support

## Tier 3 — Stretch / Context-Dependent

### Director of Operations

Requires stronger quantified leadership metrics and clearer team scope.

### Head of Operations — SMB

Strong fit for founder-led service businesses, especially where operational breadth matters more than enterprise department scale.

### Director of Customer Experience

Requires stronger customer experience metrics, team size, and service quality outcomes.

### RevOps Manager

Possible fit if CRM, funnel, sales operations, automation, and reporting experience are emphasized. Requires careful framing to avoid overstating formal RevOps experience.

## Positioning Guardrails

- Do not claim enterprise AI leadership unless tied to specific evidence.
- Do not imply formal software engineering employment unless role-specific work supports it.
- Do not overstate field technician certifications beyond actual training and work performed.
- Do not use confidential client details without explicit approval.
- Use Founder Intelligence Systems and AI Operations framing where appropriate, but keep resumes understandable to recruiters.
