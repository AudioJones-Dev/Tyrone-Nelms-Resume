# Achievement Library

Strategic curation layer over [`accomplishments.md`](accomplishments.md), which remains the factual source of truth. This file tiers achievements by evidence strength and provides reusable phrasings per audience.

Rule: this file may rephrase, tier, and target — it may not add facts. New achievements enter through `accomplishments.md` first.

## Evidence Tiers

- **Tier A — verified and specific.** Named recognition, dates, or concrete scope. Safe anywhere, including exports.
- **Tier B — verified and qualitative.** True per canonical record but without numbers. Safe anywhere; phrase qualitatively.
- **Tier C — unverified metrics.** Tracked in `accomplishments.md` → Metrics to Verify. Excluded from all public artifacts until evidence exists.

## Reusable Phrasings by Audience

### For recruiters / ATS (resume register)

- "Recognized as Employee of the Month and quarterly top performer; maintained over 99% adherence every month."
- "Trained, mentored, and coached team members; provided floor support for new hires."
- "Designed AI-supported workflows, operational documentation, and business systems for founder-led service businesses."

### For consulting prospects (plain-business register)

- "I've handled the phones, the warehouse, the field visits, and the campaigns — so the systems I build match how service businesses actually run."
- "I document workflows before automating them, so the automation survives staff changes."
- "I don't quote results I can't back; client outcomes are published only with approval and evidence."

### For speaker / bio use (third person)

- "Tyrone Nelms brings 15+ years across enterprise customer operations, field service, digital marketing, and creative production to his AI operations consulting practice, AJ Digital."
