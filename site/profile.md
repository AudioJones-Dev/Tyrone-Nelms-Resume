# Profile

## Who I Am

An operations and AI systems consultant and the founder of AJ Digital. My career stacks four layers of service-business competence: enterprise customer operations built the foundation (documentation, escalation handling, CRM discipline); operations and field roles added real-world execution; creative and digital marketing added brand and acquisition; AI-enabled systems work is the current synthesis that ties them together.

## What Problems I Solve

Service businesses running on scattered tools, undocumented processes, and reactive workflows. Typical symptoms: follow-up that depends on memory, CRM records nobody trusts, processes that live in one person's head, automation experiments that stalled, and customer experience that varies by who answers.

## Who Should Work With Me

- **Founder-led and service-based businesses** that need operations documented, systematized, and selectively automated — by someone who has personally worked support floors, warehouses, field routes, and campaigns.
- **Organizations filling senior operations, project management, or customer-experience roles** that want enterprise support discipline combined with modern systems and AI fluency.

## What Differentiates Me

1. **Operator-turned-consultant.** The advice comes from having done the jobs the systems are for.
2. **Systems over tools.** Documentation and workflow design first; tool selection second.
3. **Evidence discipline.** No claimed outcome without support — unverified numbers stay out of my materials, and client systems are built to the same standard.
4. **End-to-end service-business range.** Marketing, creative production, field operations, and back office.
