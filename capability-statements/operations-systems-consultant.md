# Capability Statement — Operations & Business Systems Consulting

## Overview

AJ Digital designs the operational backbone of service businesses: documented processes, clear workflows, usable CRM systems, and handoffs that don't leak. Led by Tyrone Nelms (see `bios/consultant-bio.md`), whose operational record spans enterprise support, office administration, warehouse, sales, and field service.

## Ideal Client

Service businesses whose growth has outrun their processes — more customers, staff, or locations than the informal system was built for — and organizations preparing for a hire, a handoff, or a tool migration that undocumented process would sabotage.

## Problems Solved

- No documented processes; quality depends on who does the work.
- Handoffs between sales, operations, and service that drop information.
- CRM used as a contact list instead of an operating system.
- Order-to-delivery or intake-to-resolution flows nobody can describe end to end.
- Team members who can't be onboarded quickly because nothing is written down.

## Approach

1. **Map the current state** — end-to-end walkthrough of the real workflow, including the workarounds.
2. **Document** — SOPs and process maps in the team's own tools, written to survive turnover.
3. **Redesign the failure points** — fix the specific handoffs and gaps the map exposes.
4. **Instrument** — make status visible (CRM stages, task systems) so management stops depending on asking around.
