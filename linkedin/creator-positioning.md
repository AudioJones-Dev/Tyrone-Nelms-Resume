# Creator Positioning

What the LinkedIn presence should become known for, and with whom. Derives from the pillars and audience framing in [`docs/career-positioning.md`](../docs/career-positioning.md).

## The One-Sentence Position

The operator who actually builds the systems: practical AI and operations content for founder-led service businesses, from someone who has worked the support floor, the warehouse, the field, and the campaigns.

## Audiences (in priority order)

1. **Founders of service businesses** (consulting pipeline) — want practical fixes, distrust hype. Content must be immediately usable.
2. **Recruiters and hiring managers** for Tier 1/2 roles (`docs/target-roles.md`) — scan for competence signals and consistency between profile and content.
3. **Peer operators and consultants** — the amplification layer; engage with their content deliberately.

## What Differentiates This Voice

- **Lived-experience specificity.** Most AI-ops content is written by people who never ran the workflows. Every post can anchor to a real floor/field/campaign memory (verified record only).
- **Evidence humility.** Openly not quoting numbers that aren't verified — in a feed full of "10x" claims, restraint is the differentiator.
- **Governed AI use.** This repository itself is proof: AI-assisted, human-gated, version-controlled. Show the method, not just the takes.

## What This Voice Does NOT Do

- No hot takes on AI news cycles without an operational angle.
- No client stories that haven't cleared the gates in `docs/case-studies.md`.
- No hype vocabulary (see banned list in `docs/professional-brand.md`).
- No pretending to be a software engineer or AI researcher — the lane is operations.

## Consistency Contract

The profile (headline, About, experience) and the content must tell the same story. Before any post: does this match the positioning pillars? If a post wouldn't make sense coming from the About section, it doesn't ship.

## Maturity Path

1. **Present** (now): profile deployed, engaging thoughtfully on others' posts.
2. **Consistent** : posting on the pillars in `content-pillars.md` at a sustainable cadence.
3. **Known-for**: inbound consulting/interview conversations reference the content.

Do not skip stages; a polished profile with no content beats inconsistent content.
