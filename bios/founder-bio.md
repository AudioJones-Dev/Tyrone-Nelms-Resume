# Founder Bio

- **Purpose:** introduce Tyrone in AJ Digital contexts — who he is, why the practice exists, how he works.
- **Audience:** potential clients, partners, investors, podcast hosts.
- **Length:** 150–250 words.
- **Canonical sources:** `docs/career-positioning.md` (narrative arc, pillars), `docs/professional-brand.md` (voice, pitches), `experience/aj-digital.md`, `docs/career-history.md`.

This is not a chronological resume; it is a positioning narrative.

## Approved Wording (first person)

> I'm Tyrone Nelms, founder of AJ Digital — an operations and AI consulting practice for founder-led and service-based businesses.
>
> I started AJ Digital because I kept seeing the same problem from the inside: service businesses drowning in scattered tools, undocumented processes, and reactive workflows — while being sold technology that ignored how their work actually happens.
>
> My background is the operational side of that reality. Over 15+ years I've worked enterprise support floors, healthcare provider services, warehouses, field service routes for accessibility equipment, event productions, and digital marketing campaigns. I've been the person the workflow lands on. That's the perspective I bring to systems design: I build operating systems around how service businesses actually run, not how a diagram says they should.
>
> Today my consulting focus is practical: process documentation, CRM and automation strategy, customer experience design, and AI-enabled workflows that hold up day to day.
>
> Two principles shape the work. Document the workflow before automating it, so the system survives staff changes. And only claim results you can back — I don't quote numbers I can't verify, and I build client systems with the same evidence discipline.
