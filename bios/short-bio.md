# Short Bio

- **Purpose:** the universal ~40-word bio for directories, event listings, article bylines, and anywhere space is tight.
- **Audience:** general — must work for clients, recruiters, and media alike.
- **Length:** ~40 words.
- **Canonical sources:** `docs/professional-brand.md` (micro bio block, title system), `docs/career-history.md` (chronology).

## Approved Wording

> Tyrone Nelms is an operations and AI consultant and founder of AJ Digital, helping founder-led service businesses turn scattered tools and reactive workflows into clear operating systems. His 15+ year background spans enterprise customer operations, field service, digital marketing, and creative production.

## Optional Wording

Trailing sentence when a credential signal helps (adds ~12 words):

> He holds Google professional certificates in Project Management, UX Design, and Digital Marketing & E-commerce.

## Open Questions

- Add the LinkedIn or portfolio URL as a byline link once confirmed (tracked in `docs/career-history.md`).

## Maintenance Rules

- Keep consistent with the micro/standard blocks in `docs/professional-brand.md`; if those change, change this the same day.
- Never extend past ~55 words — the next size up is `media-bio.md`.
