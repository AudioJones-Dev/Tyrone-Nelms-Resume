# Bios

Reusable biography variants for consulting, speaking, proposals, media, and web use. These are **not resumes** — they are positioning artifacts.

- **Facts** come only from the canonical records (`docs/career-history.md`, `docs/accomplishments.md`, `docs/certifications.md`, `experience/`).
- **Framing** comes only from the strategic layer (`docs/career-positioning.md`, `docs/professional-brand.md`, `docs/achievement-library.md`).
- The short reusable bio *blocks* (micro/standard) remain maintained in `docs/professional-brand.md`; the files here are the full deploy-ready variants built on top of them.

## Files

| File | Use | Person | Length |
|---|---|---|---|
| [`short-bio.md`](short-bio.md) | Anywhere a ~40-word bio is needed | Third | ~40 words |
| [`founder-bio.md`](founder-bio.md) | Clients, partners, podcast intros for AJ Digital contexts | First (third optional) | 150–250 words |
| [`consultant-bio.md`](consultant-bio.md) | Proposals, capability statements, RFPs, vendor profiles | Third | 100–180 words |
| [`speaker-bio.md`](speaker-bio.md) | Conferences, panels, podcasts | Third | ≤120 words |
| [`media-bio.md`](media-bio.md) | Publications, press, contributed articles | Third | 80–130 words |
| [`executive-summary.md`](executive-summary.md) | One-page overview for executive/consulting audiences | Third | 1 page |

## Rules

- No Tier C (unverified) claims — check `docs/achievement-library.md` before edits.
- No client names or engagement specifics until `docs/case-studies.md` gates clear.
- Voice: plain over impressive, evidence-humble, no hype vocabulary (`docs/professional-brand.md`).
- Edit here first, then deploy to the destination surface — same doctrine as the LinkedIn OS.
